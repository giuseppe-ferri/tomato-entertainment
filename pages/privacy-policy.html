Privacy Policy for Tomato Entertainment Services

Last Updated: August 4, 2025

Tomato Entertainment is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and disclosed across our services, including our website, https://tomatoentertainment.net/, and our mobile games, such as Flappy Astro (collectively, the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

We collect different types of information depending on which Service you use:

When you use our Games:

  • In-Game Activity: We collect data about your interactions with the Game, such as your high score, coins collected, and unlocked items. This is used to save your progress.
  • Purchase History: If you make in-app purchases, we receive a confirmation from the app store (e.g., Google Play), but we do not collect or store your payment information.
  • Device Identifiers & Diagnostics: Through our third-party partners, we collect your device's Advertising ID, crash logs, and other diagnostic data to show ads and fix bugs.

When you visit our Website:

  • Contact Information: If you contact us via email, we collect your name and email address to provide support.
  • Usage Data: We may collect non-personal information like your IP address and browser type for security and analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Improve our Services: We use gameplay data and crash logs to maintain functionality, fix bugs, and enhance the player experience.
  • Advertising: In our games, we use the Advertising ID to show you rewarded video ads. These ads are provided by our partner, Google AdMob.
  • In-App Purchases: We use purchase confirmations to unlock the content you have bought in our games.
  • Communication: We use your contact information to respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.

3. Third-Party Services

Our Services rely on trusted third-party partners. These services collect and process data under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: Used for processing in-app purchases and managing game services.
  • Firebase (from Google): We use Firebase Analytics to understand how players use our Games and Firebase Crashlytics to track and analyze crashes.
  • Google AdMob: Used to serve rewarded advertisements in our Games.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Services are intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. For advertising shown to audiences that may include children, we take steps to ensure that ads are appropriate and do not use interest-based targeting.
